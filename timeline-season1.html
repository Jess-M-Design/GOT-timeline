Season One

Locations: King's Landing, Winterfell, The Wall, Essos

  • 298 AC

    Episode 1

    "Winter Is Coming"

  • Ned Stark

    Episode 1

    Location: Winterfell

    Event: Ned Stark is introduced as the Lord of Winterfell, where he oversees the execution of a deserter from the Night’s Watch. He is with his family: Catelyn, Robb, Sansa, Arya, Bran, and Jon Snow.

  • Catelyn Stark

    Episode 1

    Location: Winterfell

    Event: Catelyn is introduced as the Lady of Winterfell and wife of Ned Stark. She receives news of Jon Arryn’s death and later convinces Ned to accept Robert Baratheon’s request to become Hand of the King.

    Event: Catelyn is devastated when Bran falls from the tower after witnessing Jaime and Cersei’s secret. She remains at his bedside, refusing to leave.

  • Robb Stark

    Episode 1

    Location: Winterfell

    Event: Welcomes King Robert Baratheon and his court to Winterfell. Helps Bran prepare for the royal arrival. Attends the feast with his family.

  • Sansa Stark

    Episode 1

    Location: Winterfell

    Event: Sansa is introduced as the eldest Stark daughter, dreaming of being queen. She is thrilled when King Robert arrives and discusses her upcoming betrothal to Prince Joffrey.

  • Arya Stark

    Episode 1

    Location: Winterfell

    Event: Arya is introduced as a tomboy who prefers archery over needlework. She watches her brothers practice and impresses Jon Snow with her skills.

  • Bran Stark

    Episode 1

    Location: Winterfell

    Event: Bran is introduced as the youngest son of **Eddard Stark** and **Catelyn Stark**. He is an enthusiastic child who admires his family, especially his father and older brother, **Robb Stark**. Bran witnesses the execution of a deserter from the Night’s Watch, marking his first experience with the harsh realities of the world. Bran also gets to ride through Winterfell on horseback with his father.
